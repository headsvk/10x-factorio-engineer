Dashboard Design Directions

Three visual identities for the 10x Factorio Engineer dashboard, each with dark + light mode.
Same content and structure — only aesthetics change.

A
Blueprint Engineering
Technical drawing meets data dashboard. Grid paper texture, engineering tick marks on progress bars,
crisp cyan ink on navy — or blueprint blue on drafting paper.
IBM Plex Mono — data & labels IBM Plex Sans — body Grid texture Square dots Cyan / blueprint-blue accent
